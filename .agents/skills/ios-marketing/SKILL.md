---
name: ios-marketing
description: Produce iOS marketing output aligned with the product, brand, localization, and App Store context.
---

When this skill is invoked:
- identify the requested marketing asset
- use product behavior and branding docs as source of truth
- adapt tone to the target market and locale
- focus on clear user benefit, differentiation, and credibility
- keep claims accurate and supportable by the product
- if relevant, produce variants for testing

Output:
- target audience
- asset type
- core message
- final copy
- optional variants
- assumptions if any

Rules:
- do not invent unsupported product claims
- do not use vague wellness clichés
- keep wording consistent with product terminology
- consider multilingual rollout and market priorities
- prefer concise, testable copy
