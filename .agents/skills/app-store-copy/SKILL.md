---
name: app-store-copy
description: Write App Store metadata and promotional copy aligned with product, locale, and ASO constraints.
---

When this skill is invoked:
- identify the requested store asset: subtitle, promotional text, description, keywords, or release notes
- align with product behavior and branding documents
- optimize for clarity, conversion, and discoverability
- adapt copy to locale and market if requested
- keep promises specific and supportable

Output:
- asset requested
- final copy
- optional variants
- notes on terminology or localization risks

Rules:
- do not invent features
- do not overpromise outcomes
- do not stuff keywords unnaturally
- keep release notes factual
- keep copy reusable for future localization
