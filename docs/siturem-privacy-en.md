# Privacy Policy — Siturem

**Last updated: April 22, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how **Siturem** processes personal data when you use the Siturem iOS application and the related pages published by **beabot.fr**, including the privacy page available at:

`https://beabot.fr/apps/siturem/#privacy`

Siturem is a structured meditation application designed for autonomous practitioners. The app is designed to operate with limited data collection.

## 1. Data Controller

**Publisher:** beabot.fr  
**Project:** Siturem  
**Contact:** hello@beabot.fr

If you have any question regarding this policy or the processing of your data, you may contact us at this address.

## 2. Data Processed

Depending on how you use the app, Siturem may process the following categories of data:

### a) App operation data
- session preferences
- selected duration
- audio settings
- gong, ambience, or reminder settings
- interface language
- local practice statistics

### b) Session data
- date and time of a session
- planned duration
- completed duration
- number of sessions
- aggregated statistics such as total practice time, 7-day or 30-day activity, current streak, or best streak

### c) Health data through HealthKit
If you enable **Apple Health / HealthKit** integration, Siturem may write data related to your mindfulness or meditation sessions to the Health app, according to the permissions you grant on your device.

Siturem does not access HealthKit without your explicit consent.

### d) Minimal technical data
Like any application, Siturem may process certain technical information strictly necessary for operation, security, error resolution, or distribution through Apple’s ecosystem.

## 3. Data Not Collected by Principle

Unless otherwise stated in this policy, Siturem is not intended to:
- require a mandatory user account
- sell your data
- use your data for advertising purposes
- use your sessions for marketing profiling
- publish your practice data
- impose invasive analytics tracking

## 4. Purposes of Processing

Your data is used only for the following purposes:
- operating the application
- saving your settings and preferences
- calculating your practice statistics
- recording a session in Apple Health if you enabled this option
- improving the stability, security, and compatibility of the application
- responding to your requests if you contact the publisher

## 5. Legal Bases

Depending on the situation, processing is based on:
- **performance of the service** requested when the data is necessary for the normal operation of the application
- **your consent** when you enable an optional feature, including writing data to HealthKit
- **legitimate interest** of the publisher in ensuring security, maintenance, and abuse prevention, limited to what is strictly necessary
- **legal obligation** where the law requires the retention or disclosure of certain information

## 6. HealthKit

When you choose to enable HealthKit:

- permission is requested by iOS
- you may accept or refuse
- the application continues to function even if you refuse
- you may later disable access in iOS settings or in the application settings, where available

HealthKit data is used only to record your sessions within Apple’s Health ecosystem.  
It is not used for advertising, marketing profiling, or sold to third parties.

## 7. Data Location and Storage

A significant portion of Siturem data is designed to be stored **locally on your device**.

If certain data is processed through technical providers strictly necessary for app distribution or operation, including Apple, such processing is subject to the terms and policies of those providers.

## 8. Data Sharing

Your data is not sold.

Data may only be shared in the following cases:
- with **Apple**, when necessary for application operation, HealthKit, in-app purchases, technical support, or App Store distribution
- with technical service providers strictly necessary for website hosting or support
- where required by law, requested by a competent authority, or necessary to protect the rights, security, or integrity of the service

## 9. Retention Period

Data is retained for a period proportionate to its purpose.

In practice:
- local preferences and statistics remain stored as long as you use the application or until they are deleted
- data written to Apple Health is also subject to your Apple environment and settings
- messages sent to support may be retained for the time necessary to process your request and manage any follow-up
- certain technical data may be retained longer if necessary for security, legal compliance, or incident resolution

## 10. Security

Reasonable measures are implemented to protect data against unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or improper disclosure.

However, no security measure offers an absolute guarantee. In the event of an incident, the response will be proportionate to the nature of the data concerned and the applicable legal obligations.

## 11. Your Rights

- right of access
- right to rectification
- right to erasure
- right to restriction of processing
- right to object
- right to data portability where applicable
- right to withdraw your consent at any time for processing based on consent

To exercise your rights, you may contact: **hello@beabot.fr**
